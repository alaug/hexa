Tutorial

1

Understand the Grid

Hexa is played on a hexagonal grid. Each cell connects to six neighboring cells.

2

Place Your Pieces

Drag and drop hexagonal pieces onto the grid. Pieces must fit within the board boundaries.

3

Clear Lines

Complete full lines in any of the three directions to clear them and score points.

4

Keep Playing

The game ends when you can no longer place any pieces. Try to achieve the highest score!
